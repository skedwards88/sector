SECTOR. Strategically place tiles to build the greatest galactic empire.
{show example tiles}
Each tile has 4 quadrants, which may be blue, red or black. Some blue and red quadrants have one of four different icons.
Take turns placing tiles in the Expanse (black area).
{show adj ok, overlap ok, diag not ok}
At least one quadrant of the placed tile must be adjacent to or overlapping a quadrant of another tile in the Expanse.
{show blue over red not ok}
Quadrants may overlap, except blue cannot overlap red and visa versa.
{show example scores}
A sector is any connected set of quadrants of your color. A sector is worth 1 point per quadrant, plus 1 point per unique icon.
{show button to score}
Once per game, a player can score points for their highest scoring sector.
